Dragon Ball Super


MAL Score: 7.46


Seven years after the events of Dragon Ball Z, Earth is at peace, and its people
live free from any dangers lurking in the universe. However, this peace is short-lived;
a sleeping evil awakens in the dark reaches of the galaxy: Beerus, the ruthless God of
Destruction. Disturbed by a prophecy that he will be defeated by a "Super Saiyan God,"
Beerus and his angelic attendant Whis start searching the universe for this mysterious being.
Before long, they reach Earth where they encounter SON-Goku, one of the planet's mightiest warriors,
and his similarly powerful friends. [Written by MAL Rewrite]


Story

Dragon Ball Z follows Goku and his friends as they defend Earth from powerful enemies like Saiyans, Frieza, Cell, and Majin Buu.

Saiyan Transformations

The series is famous for Goku’s and Vegeta’s Super Saiyan forms—each transformation bringing massive power boosts and iconic moments.

Battles & Training

DBZ focuses heavily on intense battles, power levels, martial arts, and tough training to push characters beyond their limits.

Themes

Dragon Ball Z highlights strength, courage, protecting loved ones, teamwork, and never giving up, inspiring fans worldwide.
